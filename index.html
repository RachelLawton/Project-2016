My Project idea:

My project is a device the measures the amount of water in a pets bowl and sends a tweet to the owner when the bowl is empty or full.

How my idea Works

My Project is based on the resistance of water. Water can conduct electricity but not very well making it a insulator,Insulators are high in resistance. The resistance is then picked up by my water sensor. This information is then sent to my Galileo through pin 2. This valuable information is then sent to the Wyliodrin platform where its is used by my code that I created. Ny code then determines what tweet to send to the owner based on the resistance given. If it receives a high resistance then the pet bowl is full of water, If there is a low resistance then the bowl is empty.

Technology used:

In my Work i used: 1 Intel Galileo Gen2 2 Bread board 3 Wyliodrin 4 Twitter 5 Github
